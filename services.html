Our services

Drone surveys & inspections for every trade at height

Whatever you need to see on a roof, facade or rooftop, we capture it safely from the air — and hand you imagery, measurements and reports you can act on straight away.

Roof inspections

Find every fault before you quote

We capture the entire roof in ultra-high detail — ridges, valleys, flashing, chimneys and gutters — so you can identify damage, plan repairs and quote accurately without ever setting foot on a ladder.

  • Slipped, cracked & missing tiles

    Zoomed, annotated stills of every problem area.

  • Flashing, valleys & gutters

    Identify blockages and water-ingress risks early.

  • Insurance & storm-damage reports

    Dated, evidenced imagery for claims and clients.

Solar panel surveys

Plan installs and prove performance

For installers and repair crews: assess a roof's suitability before quoting, plan the panel layout, and run radiometric thermal scans after install to catch faults that kill output.

  • Pre-install roof assessment

    Pitch, orientation, shading and condition at a glance.

  • Thermal fault detection

    Find hotspots, cracked cells and dead strings fast.

  • Maintenance & warranty evidence

    Document array health for clients and manufacturers.

HVAC & rooftop plant

Survey commercial roofs without the climb

Maintenance engineers and HVAC contractors use our aerial surveys to inspect rooftop units, condensers and ducting, plan works and assess access on large or awkward commercial roofs.

  • Unit condition & access checks

    See plant layout and condition before you mobilise.

  • Thermal performance scans

    Identify failing units and insulation issues.

  • Method-statement support

    Visual evidence for safe-system-of-work planning.

Building & facade surveys

Measured imagery for architects & cleaners

Capture full elevations, fine detail and 3D models of any structure. Perfect for architects working up existing-condition drawings and for facade-cleaning crews scoping access and surfaces.

  • High-detail elevations

    Every window, joint and crack, captured to scale.

  • 3D models & photogrammetry

    Measurable models for design and planning.

  • Condition & cleaning scoping

    Assess surfaces and access before mobilising.
