FAQ

Common questions

How much does a drone survey cost?

It depends on the site, the type of survey and what deliverables you need. Tell us the job and we'll send a clear, fixed quote with no obligation — usually the same working day.

Are you insured and IAA-compliant?

Yes. We operate as fully insured, qualified remote pilots in line with Irish Aviation Authority requirements, with the right permissions for the work we carry out.

How quickly will I get the imagery and report?

Most surveys are turned around within 24–48 hours of the flight. If you're working to a tight deadline, let us know and we'll do our best to prioritise it.

What areas do you cover?

We're based in Ireland and survey nationwide. Travel for sites outside our immediate area can be included in your quote.

Can you fly in poor weather?

Safety and image quality come first, so very high winds or heavy rain can mean rescheduling. We keep an eye on the forecast and will always agree the best window with you in advance.
