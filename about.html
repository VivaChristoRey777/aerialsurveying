About us

Putting a safer set of eyes on every job at height

We're a drone surveying service built for the trades — helping contractors, installers, engineers and architects inspect at height without the cost, risk or disruption of traditional access.

Our story

Practical aerial data for real-world trades

Aerial Surveying was founded on a simple idea: most inspections at height are slow, expensive and risky — and they don't have to be. A drone in the hands of a qualified pilot can capture more detail in twenty minutes than a day on scaffold, and put it straight into the hands of the people who need it.

We work alongside roofing contractors, solar installers, HVAC and maintenance engineers, facade and power-washing crews, and architects. Whatever your trade, we speak your language and deliver imagery and reports you can act on — and hand to your own clients.
